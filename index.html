  • First Date

    August 1, 2021

    After almost 7 months of long distance, we finally met in Canada. After my hotel quarantine period, Melwyn came to visit me for the first time. I was looking out the hotel window & searching eagerly for his car. ... When he reached, I ran down the stairs with butterflies in my stomach. I approached towards him as he got out of his car with a bouquet of flowers in his hand. He had the sweetest, most genuine smile on his face & I still have an exact memory of it to this day. Melwyn & I ended up spending the rest of the day together just chatting and getting to know one another even better. We had pretty much everything in common. We were both obsessed with movies, and our favorites were the same. Same taste in music, same favorite bands & a few of the same favorite songs. He was checking every single thing on my compatibility list, not to mention he was incredibly funny. I had honestly never been that smitten with anyone in my life. Tall, tan skin, dark hair, gorgeous brown eyes. Check, check, check & check! Besides having everything in common & having a ridiculously beautiful smile, he was most importantly, kind. I believe you can tell when someone is genuinely nice, kind & respectful within minutes. Melwyn was a gentleman and on top of all that, he had a great sense of humor. ​ -Riyanna
